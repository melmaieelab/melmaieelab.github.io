  • Richmond won first place in the Plant Breeding and Data Analytics Hackathon (2024) and was recognized as an Encompass Fellow (2024), sponsored by Bayer Crop Science.

  • Nefertiti Griffin has been selected as the Marie Clark Taylor Fellow for the 2024-2025 academic year. A fellowship named after Dr. Marie Clark Taylor, the first Black woman to earn a PhD in Botany. Through this fellowship, she had the incredible opportunity to attend two amazing conferences in Lincoln, Nebraska: the 2025 McFadden Symposium for Wheat Research and the Nebraska Plant Science Symposium.
